CHALLENGES FACING THE ENVIRONMENT

Pollution:

Contamination of air, water, and soil by harmful substances.

Deforestation:

The loss of forests due to human activities like logging and agriculture.

Climate Change:

Global warming caused by excessive greenhouse gas emissions.

Biodiversity Loss:

Extinction of species due to habitat destruction and human interference.

Resource Depletion:

Overuse of natural resources leading to scarcity and ecological imbalance.
